International Water Management Institute (IWMI)

IWMI is a non-profit, scientific research organization focusing on the sustainable use of water and land resources in developing countries. We work to improve the lives of poor people by providing evidence-based solutions to sustainably manage water and land resources.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Water, climate change and resilience
  • Water Data Science
  • Water, food and ecosystems
  • Water, growth and inclusion
