üåü Overview

The Solar Suitability Dashboard is an advanced geospatial analytics platform designed to assess solar energy potential and suitability across Indian districts and states.

üéØ Purpose

This dashboard helps policymakers, researchers, and stakeholders make informed decisions about solar energy deployment by providing comprehensive analysis across three key objectives:

Adaptation

Climate resilience strategies

Mitigation

GW Sustainability

Groundwater conservation
